Server Rules

🔴 Violation of these rules may lead to warnings, kicks, temporary bans, or permanent bans.
✅ Our priority is immersive, serious, and respectful roleplay. Understanding these rules ensures a better experience for everyone.

1️⃣ No RDM (Random Deathmatch)

🔫 Killing someone without a valid roleplay reason is strictly forbidden. You must have a legitimate in-character reason and clear build-up before engaging in violence.

🚫 KOS (Kill on Sight) is only allowed in specific gang wars or approved by staff ahead of time.

Punishment: ⚠️ Warning → 📞 Staff Review → 🕐 1-Day Ban → ⏳ 3-Day Ban → 🔒 Perm Ban

2️⃣ No VDM (Vehicle Deathmatch)

🚗 Intentionally using a vehicle to run over players without proper RP is not allowed.

Punishment: ⚠️ Warning → 📞 Staff Review → 🕑 2-Day Ban → ⏳ 7-Day Ban → 🔒 Perm Ban

3️⃣ No Fail RP

🎭 You must behave and react realistically to your character’s situation (e.g., ignoring injuries, pulling large weapons from nowhere).

Punishment: ⚠️ Warning → 📞 Staff Review → 📘 RP Training → ⏳ 3-Day Ban → 🔒 Perm Ban

4️⃣ No Metagaming

🧠 Using OOC knowledge in-character is not allowed (e.g., watching streams to track players, sharing info via Discord).

Punishment: ⚠️ Warning → 📞 Staff Review → 🕐 1-Day Ban → ⏳ 3-Day Ban

5️⃣ No Powergaming

🤼 Don’t force actions on others or RP unrealistically (e.g., “/me knocks you out” with no chance to react).

Punishment: ⚠️ Warning → 📞 Staff Review → 🕑 2-Day Ban → ⏳ 7-Day Ban

6️⃣ Show Respect (IC & OOC)

🤝 No racism, hate speech, or harassment of any kind. Personal attacks will result in instant action.

Punishment: ❌ Immediate Kick or Ban → 📞 Staff Review

7️⃣ No Combat Logging

🚪 You must stay in-character, even when things aren’t going your way. Do not log off mid-arrest or shootout.

Punishment: ⚠️ Warning → 📞 Staff Review → 🕐 1-Day Ban → ⏳ 3-Day Ban → 🔒 Perm Ban

8️⃣ Use Realistic Names

🧍 No meme, celebrity, or troll names (e.g., Big Chungus, Lil Skrrt420).

Punishment: 🔄 Character Reset → ⚠️ Warning → 📞 Staff Review → ⏳ Temp Ban

9️⃣ No Cheating or Exploiting

💻 Hacking, glitches, and exploits will result in zero tolerance.

Punishment: 🚫 Immediate Permanent Ban

🔟 Use Common Sense

🧭 If it feels wrong or unrealistic—don’t do it. Do not abuse rule loopholes.

Punishment: 📞 Staff Discretion → ⚠️ Case-by-case

1️⃣1️⃣ Respect Female Characters & Players

👩‍🦰 Harassment, sexual RP, or disrespect will not be tolerated.

Punishment: ⚠️ Warning → 📞 Staff Review → ❌ Kick → ⏳ Temp Ban → 🔒 Perm Ban

1️⃣2️⃣ Respect Male Characters & Players

👨 All players are protected from harassment equally.

Punishment: ⚠️ Warning → 📞 Staff Review → ❌ Kick → ⏳ Temp Ban → 🔒 Perm Ban

1️⃣3️⃣ No Force RP

🛑 Do not force players into RP they are trying to avoid.

Punishment: ⚠️ Warning → 📞 Staff Review → 🕑 2-Day Ban → ⏳ 7-Day Ban

1️⃣4️⃣ NLR – New Life Rule

🪦 After death, forget everything and wait 15 minutes to return to the scene.

Punishment: 📞 Staff Review → 🧑‍⚖️ Case-by-case

1️⃣5️⃣ No Stealing Miami Coin Guns

🪙 These are exclusive items, not regular loot.

Punishment: ⚠️ Warning → 📞 Staff Review → ❌ Kick → ⏳ Temp Ban → 🔒 Perm Ban

1️⃣6️⃣ Speed Limit Rule

🏎️ Speeds over 250 MPH are not allowed.

Punishment: ⚠️ Warning → 📞 Staff Review → 🚘 Vehicle Confiscation → ⏳ Temp Ban

1️⃣7️⃣ Police Scene Engagement Rule

👮 You must RP for at least 30 seconds before attacking law enforcement.

Punishment: ⚠️ Warning → 📞 Staff Review → 🕐 1-Day Ban → ⏳ 3-Day Ban → 🔒 Perm Ban

1️⃣8️⃣ No Cop Baiting

🚔 Don't provoke police without RP purpose (e.g., doing donuts outside PD, ramming cruisers for fun).

Punishment: ⚠️ Warning → 📞 Staff Review → 🕐 1-Day Ban → ⏳ 3-Day Ban → 🔒 Perm Ban

1️⃣9️⃣ No Stealing EMS Vehicles

🚑 EMS vehicles may not be stolen under any circumstances, unless part of an approved, realistic RP scenario.

Punishment: ⚠️ Warning → 📞 Staff Review → 🚘 Vehicle Confiscation → ⏳ Temp Ban

2️⃣0️⃣ Ignoring Staff Rule

🚨 If a staff member gives you a direction, you must follow it. Arguing during a sit or ignoring messages is a serious offense.

Punishment: ⚠️ Warning → 📞 Staff Review → 🕑 2-Day Ban → ⏳ 7-Day Ban → 🔒 Perm Ban

2️⃣1️⃣ Water Dump Rule

🌊 Throwing downed players into water to drown/kill them is not allowed, as it makes EMS/PD RP impossible.

Punishment: ⚠️ Warning → 📞 Staff Review → ⏳ 3-Day Ban → 🔒 Perm Ban

2️⃣2️⃣ Server Disrespect (Server Diss)

💬 Speaking negatively about the server or trying to recruit people to other communities is prohibited.

Punishment: ⚠️ Warning → 📞 Staff Review → ❌ Kick → ⏳ Temp Ban → 🔒 Perm Ban

📢 Final Reminder

Miami Life RP is a serious RP environment. We want everyone to enjoy immersive, meaningful stories — together. Respect the rules, respect your fellow players, and build your legacy with integrity.
