Server Rules

🔫

1. No RDM (Random Deathmatch)

Don't kill other players without a valid RP reason.

Punishment: ⚠️ Warning → 🕐 1-day ban → ⏳ 3-day ban → 🔒 Permanent ban

🚗

2. No VDM (Vehicle Deathmatch)

Do not run people over unless it’s valid RP (e.g., fleeing danger).

Punishment: ⚠️ Warning → 🕑 2-day ban → ⏳ 7-day ban → 🔒 Permanent ban

🎭

3. No Fail RP

Don’t do unrealistic things (e.g., pulling guns from nowhere, ignoring injuries).

Punishment: ⚠️ Warning → 📘 RP Training → ⏳ 3-day ban → 🔒 Permanent ban

🧠

4. No Metagaming

Using OOC info IC (e.g., reading Discord DMs or streams) is not allowed.

Punishment: ⚠️ Warning → 🕐 1-day ban → ⏳ 3-day ban

🤼

5. No Powergaming

Forcing actions on others without giving them a chance to respond is unfair.

Punishment: ⚠️ Warning → 🕑 2-day ban → ⏳ 7-day ban

🤝

6. Respect Staff & Players

No harassment, racism, hate speech, or trolling. Be respectful always.

Punishment: ❌ Immediate kick or ban (based on severity)

🚪

7. No Combat Logging

Logging out during RP to avoid consequences (jail, death, etc.) is not allowed.

Punishment: 🕐 1-day ban → ⏳ 3-day ban → 🔒 Permanent ban

🧍

8. Realistic Character Names

Use realistic names – no memes, celebrities, or troll names (e.g., “LilSkrrt” or “Goku420”).

Punishment: 🔄 Character reset → ⚠️ Warning → ⏳ Temp ban

💻

9. No Exploiting or Cheating

Using glitches, hacks, or mods for advantage = instant PERM BAN.

Punishment: 🚫 No warnings → 🔥 Permanent ban

🧭

10. Use Common Sense

If it feels wrong, don’t do it. Help build RP – not destroy it.

Punishment: 🧑‍⚖️ Staff discretion (case-by-case)

👩

11. No Disrespect Toward Female Players or Characters

Disrespect, harassment, or inappropriate behavior toward women — whether in-character or out-of-character — will not be tolerated.

Punishment: ⚠️ Warning → ❌ Kick → ⏳ Temp Ban → 🔒 Permanent ban
